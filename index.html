About CalPo.

What's CalPo.?

Based on [Wikipedia] writes on https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polyhedron, in geometry, Polyhedron is three-dimensional shape with flat polygonal faces, straight edges and sharp corners or vertices.

CalPo. is a simple calculator that will help you to solve your mathematics problem about Polyhedron. It is not only focus to calculate the solutions of Polyhedron's surface area and volume, but also edge, lenght, widht, height, slant height, etc.
